• The title of the essay should be Whatgoingtouniversitywouldmeantome.

B3.7 Study skills: Focus on speaking
A Register
The word register has several meanings. This is one of them:
1
Work with a partner and read the two short dialogues below and compare them.
Dialogue 1
Student A: Hi! You okay?
Student B: Yeah, I’m okay. And you?
Student A: Yeah, fine. But I’ve got to go now. Bye.
Student B: See you.
Dialogue 2
Student A: Good morning! How are you today?
Student B: I’m very well thank you, and you?
Student A: I’m fine too, thank you very much. I’m sorry but I’m in a terrible hurry, will you
excuse me?
Student B: Yes, of course. I hope I’ll see you again soon.
2
What is the register of the two dialogues above?
a Polite and formal used between people who are not on socially familiar terms.
b Informal used between people who are on socially familiar terms.
c Neutral
