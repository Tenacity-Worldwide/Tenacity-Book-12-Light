A10.3 Language focus: Narrative tenses
When you write a story, a biography or an account of an event, you are writing a narrative.
A narrative is a description of events in the past. Therefore, past tense verb forms are used
in narratives. Although present tense verb forms may also be used to relate the events to the
present.
