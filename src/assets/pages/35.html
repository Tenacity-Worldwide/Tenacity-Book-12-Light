The Girl from Abroad. Read it and then do these tasks.
1 Choose the best answers to these questions

a A short distance.
b Quite a long way.
c We don’t know.

b Because they didn’t like him.
c Because in their culture being late was not acceptable.

a Because they could see them over the hedge.
b Because they already knew they were few.
c Because of the sound of the horns.

a It was traditional for the best man to go and get the bride.

mud.
