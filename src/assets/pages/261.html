B11.1 Language focus:Indirectorembeddedquestions
When asking for information, particularly when it is of a personal nature, we often use indirect
orembeddedquestions.
Comparethesepairsofquestions:
•

 • Are your parents still living?(Yes/Noquestion)

Otherpoliterequestswecanuseinindirectquestionsare:

 • Would you tell me …?
