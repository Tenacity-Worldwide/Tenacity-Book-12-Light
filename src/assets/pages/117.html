A5.7 Writing: A good leader
1
Work with a partner. Make a list of the contexts in which there are leaders. For example, a
class leader, a president and so on.
2
Discuss to what extent you think all leaders need the same qualities.
3
Now on your own, write a paragraph about what makes a good leader. The paragraph must
express your own opinion. You should also justify the points you make and provide examples.
When you have finished, check your paragraph carefully and make any necessary changes.
4
Read paragraphs written by other students in your class and compare your ideas.
