A11.2 Listening: The Ethiopian film industry
1
Do you watch Ethiopian films? Which ones have you particularly enjoyed?
What do you know about the film industry in this country?
2
You are going to listen to a talk about the Ethiopian Film Industry. As you listen, make
outline notes. To review how to make outline notes, see A3.3.
3
After the first listening, check your notes with a partner. Listen to the text again and add
more information to your outline.
