2
Practise the pronunciation of the nouns and adjectives. Mark the stressed syllables in each
word.
3
Go through the list and discuss the extent to which you possess each of these skills and
qualities. Also discuss what sorts of jobs these qualities would be useful for, such as doctor,
teacher and so on.
Example:
Student A: I don’t really have an eye for detail. I often overlook things and make silly mistakes.
Student B: Do you think you could train yourself to be good with details?
Student A: Maybe, to some extent. But I think I’m better at seeing the bigger picture.
