B7.9 Study skills: Focus on reading
A Predicting
Thinking about what a text is going to be about is a very useful way of helping you to
understand it. For example: just looking at the title, the way the text is organised and any sub-
headings can give you a good idea of the content. In addition, predicting is something you can
do as you read each paragraph and each sentence.
