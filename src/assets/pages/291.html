B11.8 Changing the subject
Student A
Stage 1
Ask student B his/her views on these discussion topics.
• Try to discuss each one for a couple of minutes.
• If B changes the subject, you should bring the subject back to your discussion topic.
1 Do you think the government should provide money for film makers in Ethiopia or
should it be a low priority?
2 Does violence in films have a bad effect on children?
3 Should films which contain nudity be shown in Ethiopia?
4 Do films generally do more good than harm?
Stage 2
Student B is going to ask you your views on some discussion topics. You must discuss them with
Student B but also try to change the subject to your three secret topics, as often as you can.
Your three secret topics are:
• the film Titanic
• another film you have seen and liked
• your family.
Use relevant language for changing the subject and coming back to the subject.
