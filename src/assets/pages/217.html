B9.8 Speaking: Preserve it or abolish it?
The theme of this unit is tradition versus progress. We have looked at the values of our traditions and the
need to adapt to the modern world. We have also looked at some of the risks of development. In order
to reconcile the old with the new we are now going to look critically at our traditions.
1
Work in a group and list some of your local traditional practices and customs.
2

abolished. Before you start, look at the language in the box.
