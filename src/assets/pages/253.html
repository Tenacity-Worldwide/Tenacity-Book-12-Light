A11.7 Language focus: Reported speech
Tense changes in reported speech
In reported speech, the tenses used can vary according to when something is reported, for
example:
 • Hirut said, “I like ice-cream”.
This is always true so we can report it:
Hirut said she likes ice-cream.
 • Sefanit said yesterday “I am going to town later.”
The event has now past so we report it:
Sefanit said yesterday that she was going to town later.
