B5.3 Language focus: Talking about the future
Will be (doing)
• In B4.3 we looked at how we can talk about future events using will (do) and will have (done).
• Another structure is will be (doing) which is used to talk about events that will be going on at
a certain time in the future.
For example:
It is predicted that by 2050 millions more people in the world will be living in
poverty unless the MDGs are achieved.
