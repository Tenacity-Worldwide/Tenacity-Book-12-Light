A Participle adjectives
• A large group of adjectives are verb participles, with an -ing or -ed ending. For example,
interesting, interested. These are frequently used to describe appearance, character and
feelings.
• Adjectives with an -ing ending describe something.
• Adjectives with an -ed ending describe the effect that thing has on you.
Example:
The lesson was interesting. We were all interested in what we were doing.
