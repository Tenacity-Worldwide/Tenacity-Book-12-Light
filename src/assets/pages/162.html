A7.4 Increase your word power: Adjectives with the suffixes -ful and -less
Read these definitions of the suffixes -ful and -less.
-ful /fəl/suffix [in adjectives]
1 full of something: an eventful day
2 having the quality of something or causing something: Restful colours / Is it painful?
-less /ləs/suffix [in adjectives]
1 without something: I felt powerless / A childless couple
2 not doing or using something: You’re too careless
3 not possible to treat or affect in a particular way: On countless occasions
-ful and -less are often used with the same words to form opposites. However, this is not always
the case, for example, ‘awful’ but not ‘awless’.
