B8.4 Speaking: Weak vowels and weak forms
Weak vowels
Sometimes unstressed syllables are very weak. The vowel is weakened to either /ı/ or /ə/.
For example: the u in minute is pronounced /ı/ and the first a and the last a in banana are
pronounced /ə/.
