These expressions are called fillers. They are called this because we use them to fill the spaces
when we are thinking about what to say or the words to use.
Example:
Student A: What’s the capital of India?
Student B: Er… Let me think. Umm… It’s umm… Mumbai, no it isn’t! Umm. It’s …
What’s it called? I know… it’s Delhi! Yes, Delhi!
