Prepare an outline of your plan for the New United Nations.
Think of some of the pressing issues that the New United Nations needs to deal with. These can be
political, economic and humanitarian. Try to use sentences with it’s time … (see B5.9) when putting
forward points. Make a list of your ideas.

Prepare a short presentation of your plans for the New United Nations.
Try to include as many members of the group as possible in your presentation. When everyone is ready,
each group should give their presentation to the class.
