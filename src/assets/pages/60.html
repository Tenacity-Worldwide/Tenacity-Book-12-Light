A3.3 Reading: Notes on study skills
1
Work in a small group. In your group you are going to read one of the texts below on an
important area of study skills. Your teacher will tell you which text to read.
2
Discuss the text in your group.
• Did you understand all the points in the text?
• Can you think of a practical example of how you could apply what you have read?
