B6.11 Writing: A business letter
You have seen that Fair Trade helps producers to get a fair price for their goods.
Imagine that you have heard that a food processing company in the UK is using Ethiopian
sesame seeds in the manufacture of tahini (a product similar to peanut butter but made
with sesame seeds). It is not Fair Trade, however.
Write a letter to the Managing Director to persuade him/her to participate in a Fair Trade
scheme for the purchase of sesame seeds.
