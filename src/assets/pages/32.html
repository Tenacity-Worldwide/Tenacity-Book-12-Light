B1.11 Study skills: Recording vocabulary
Whether in your mother tongue or a second language, there are always new words and
expressions to learn. So that you don’t forget them, it is useful to record them when you come
across them, for example in class or when you are reading at home. In order to do this, it is a
good idea to have a separate vocabulary notebook, which you can carry easily in your
school bag.
However, there are three important decisions to make:
•
How are you going to organise your vocabulary book?
•
What are you going to record about your new words and expressions?
•
How are you going to use it effectively?
There are several ways of doing these tasks, some of which you have looked at in earlier grades.
