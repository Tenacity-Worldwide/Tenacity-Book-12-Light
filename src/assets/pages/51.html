B2.2 Language focus: Talking about past events
When we talk about inventions, we often use the passive. When talking about the history of
inventions we may need to use a variety of past verb forms in the passive, for example: past
simple, past continuous, past perfect and present perfect.
Compare these sentences:
• Human speech developed 200,000 years ago. (active)
• The first writing system was developed around 4000 BC. (passive)
